Why Willy Wonka's My Fav Movie

Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory, the old version with Gene Wilder is my favourite movie for many reasons. However, it is attached to a memory which I'm very fond of. I remember watching it with my Nanny when we lived in the old trailer park in Falmouth when I was a kid. I remember the flowing chocolate entry scene like it was yesterday, and how mesmerized I had been as a child. Even still, I continue to love the movie; here is a few more reasons..

  • It is a feel good movie
  • The whole movie is about chocolate and candy, how exciting
  • Karma gets the little devil children and we love to see that
  • The close relationship Charlie has with Grandpa Joe makes me warm and fuzzy remembering my Nanny
